AJA Digital Recorders

Ki Pro's (pronounced key-pro) are AJA's (pronounced A-J-A) device to record high quality low loss video. They come in different flavors with different inputs and form factors. The current models all can record multiple inputs and have web GUI's.

Ki Pro Ultra 12G

The Ultra 12G Is AJA's 4K recorder. It will record multiple 12G-SDI inputs onto its PAK Media cards. It will also take in analog audio via a breakout cable if you don't have audio embedded on the SDI. Video can also be imputed via Fiber (SMPTE-297). HDMI, timecode, and Reference inputs are available. You will find this recorder in many small, and mid sized truck's, studio's, mobile racks and fly packs.
